Second Languaje Teaching

Our History

One of our most beloved activity is teaching second languages with technology. Our first project was charged to us after earning a bidding for an autonomous English program at PDVSA, the main oil refinery in the Venezuela of the 90’s.

Subsequently after effectively maintaining our own language academy in our offices in the CCCT, Caracas, and then at Santa Paula, Caracas, we were hired by EL COLEGIO EMIL FRIEDMAN, Caracas to design, manage and deliver a semi-autonomous English Program with heavy use of the latest computer technology, in the same tradition that started our language teaching philosophy. Computerized tests were added to our know-how. High schoolers were tested in their language laboratory and test scores were received immediately after they submitted their responses. Relocation to the US had already started and teaching English and Spanish is part of our routine now.

Trados Studio Training

Why Trados Studio training?

It is unlikely that a successful translator can run free for too long before he or she receives a call from an agency asking for somebody with good knowledge and familiarity with one CAT tool. Trados Studio is the one we know best and the one we are investing the most to learn its many features.

When issues arise that we cannot solve, we rely on consultants to help out. Are you a beginner? We can help. We can accommodate single or group classes. Contact us for details.
